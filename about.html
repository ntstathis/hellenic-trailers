About Hellenic Trailers

Your trusted partner for Lamberet refrigerated transport in Greece

Three Industry Leaders, One Vision

Hellenic Trailers was founded by three leading companies in the Greek refrigerated transport industry — Stathis, Theologis Group, and Systems SA — who joined forces to become the official representative of Lamberet in Greece. By combining decades of expertise in vehicle body-building, refrigeration systems, and after-sales service, Hellenic Trailers offers an unparalleled level of quality and support to its customers.

Based at the state-of-the-art Theologis Group facilities in Mandra, Attica — a 15,000 m² service center — Hellenic Trailers brings together the best of each founding company to deliver complete cold chain solutions: from the sale of new Lamberet vehicles, to Carrier Transicold refrigeration units, to expert maintenance, bodywork, and genuine spare parts.

Our Founding Companies

Three pillars of excellence in refrigerated transport

Stathis

Since 1961, Stathis has been one of the leading companies in vehicle body-building and refrigerated bodies in Greece.

  • Vehicle body-building & refrigerated bodies since 1961
  • Facilities in Thessaloniki & Athens
  • Transport refrigeration, tail lifts & fleet management

Theologis Group

Founded in 1980 by Theologos Thanasis, the Theologis Group has established itself as a reliable and innovative force in refrigerated vehicle repairs and special constructions.

  • 40+ years in repairs & special constructions
  • 15,000 m² facility in Mandra, Attica
  • Chassis, bodywork, refrigeration & cold stores

Systems SA

Established in 1990, Systems SA holds the exclusive distribution rights in Greece for Carrier Transicold, Dhollandia, and Webasto.

  • Exclusive distributor: Carrier Transicold, Dhollandia, Webasto
  • Nationwide service network
  • Factory-trained engineers & technical support
